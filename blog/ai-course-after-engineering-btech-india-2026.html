AI after B.Tech — two tracks to understand

B.Tech engineers pursuing AI in 2026 fall into two very different tracks. Technical track: becoming an ML Engineer, AI Engineer, Data Scientist — requires strong Python, mathematics, and ML framework skills. The IIT technical certificates (Madras, Roorkee, Guwahati) are designed for this. Management track: leading AI implementations as Head of AI, CDAO, or VP Engineering — requires understanding AI strategy without necessarily coding. The IIT Kanpur CDAO and IIM Indore programmes are for this track. Know which you're targeting before picking a programme.
