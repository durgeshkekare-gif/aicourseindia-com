Generative AI Career in India 2026 — Complete Jobs & Salary Guide

GenAI job market in India — 2026 snapshot

Generative AI jobs in India grew by over 300% between 2023 and 2026. Three forces are driving this: enterprises deploying GenAI for productivity (every company with 500+ employees has at least one AI initiative now), the DPDP Act creating compliance demand for AI governance roles, and Indian startups building AI-native products for global markets. The result: a genuine talent shortage at the GenAI engineer, AI product, and AI governance layers.

Top companies hiring GenAI talent in India 2026

Highest-paying GenAI roles: Anthropic India (building local team), Google DeepMind India, Microsoft AI, Amazon Science. Strong GenAI hiring: Infosys AI unit, TCS AI.Cloud, Wipro AI, HCL AI. FinTech: Razorpay, PhonePe, CRED, Zepto AI teams. Startups: Sarvam AI, Krutrim, Ola Krutrim, Pika, Murf.ai, Invideo AI. Enterprise AI roles: HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Bajaj Finance AI teams.

The fast path to a GenAI career — 6-month roadmap

  • Month 1: Python basics (free — NPTEL, YouTube) + DeepLearning.AI free prompt engineering course
  • Month 2–3: Build first RAG project (connect a PDF to ChatGPT using LangChain — tutorials on YouTube). Deploy on Streamlit. Push to GitHub.
  • Month 4–6: Enroll in IIT Madras GenAI & LLMs certificate for structured learning + IIT credential. Build capstone project.
  • Month 7: Apply for GenAI Engineer or ML Engineer roles. Your GitHub projects + IIT Madras certificate is a competitive combination.
