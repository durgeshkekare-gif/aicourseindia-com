IIT AI Courses India 2026 — Complete Guide for Working Professionals

Why IIT AI programs are different in 2026

The market for AI courses in India is crowded — Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning, and dozens of bootcamps all offer AI certificates. What makes IIT programs different is institutional credibility. An IIT Bombay or IIT Roorkee certificate on your profile is immediately recognised by Indian employers as a rigorous, faculty-led credential — not a self-paced online course.

In 2026, five IITs offer AI and Data Science certificate programs for working professionals through weekend online delivery: IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Roorkee, IIT Madras and IIT Guwahati. No JEE required.

How to choose the right IIT AI program

If you're non-technical (manager, business leader): IIT Bombay's AI for Business Managers is the only program built without assuming any coding background. It focuses on GenAI strategy, prompt engineering and enterprise AI adoption.

If you want the most rigorous technical program: IIT Madras (IITM Pravartak) Advanced Data Science — 10 months, research-led, deep learning focused, from NIRF #1.

If you want a campus IIT experience: IIT Roorkee includes 2 campus immersion modules at the Roorkee campus with accommodation provided.

If you want a global credential: IIT Madras + Rotman School (University of Toronto) is the only program that gives you both an IIT and a globally ranked business school credential at ₹1.61L.
