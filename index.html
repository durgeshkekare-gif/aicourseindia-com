Why it matters

Why IIT or IIM for your AI course

01
Credential employers recognise
An IIT Bombay or IIM Kozhikode certificate on your profile carries weight no online platform certificate does. Hiring managers know exactly what it means.
02
Faculty who actually research AI
IIT professors are active researchers in AI, ML, NLP and deep learning — not instructors reading slides. The curriculum reflects what's actually happening in the field.
03
Campus immersion — real IIT experience
Most programs include 2–5 day campus modules at the actual IIT. You attend sessions in IIT Delhi's or IIT Roorkee's facility — not just online.
04
No JEE or CAT required
These are executive education programs — admission is based on your professional profile, not entrance exams. Any graduate with work experience can apply.
05
Cohort of senior professionals
Your peers are managers, engineers and analysts from established companies — not freshers. The peer learning and network is as valuable as the content.
06
Weekend delivery — keep your job
All programs are structured for working professionals. Live sessions on Saturday and Sunday, recordings available. No career break required.

Frequently asked questions

It depends on the program. IIT technical programs (IIT Delhi, IIT Roorkee, IIT Madras Advanced DS) require comfort with basic programming and mathematics. IIT Bombay's AI for Business Managers and IIM programs are designed for non-technical professionals — no coding background required. Check the specific program's eligibility section.
For career advancement in India specifically — yes. An IIT certificate carries institutional credibility that platform certificates (Coursera, Udemy, even Google) do not in most Indian corporate environments. The peer cohort, live faculty interaction, campus module and alumni network are also meaningfully different. For pure knowledge acquisition, online platforms are fine. For career positioning, IIT/IIM credentials have a clear edge.
Three programs are specifically designed for non-technical professionals: (1) IIT Bombay AI for Business Managers — GenAI strategy and enterprise AI adoption; (2) IIM Kozhikode Applied Analytics & GenAI — business decision-making with data; (3) IIM Indore AI & Cybersecurity — AI strategy with a security focus. None of these require programming experience.
Each IIT has a distinct focus. IIT Madras (IITM Pravartak) has the strongest deep learning and GenAI research base. IIT Delhi's program is mathematics-heavy — strong for those who want rigorous ML foundations. IIT Roorkee's program is the most practical with 2 campus immersions. IIT Bombay's program is uniquely suited for business managers. The "best" depends entirely on your background and goal.
All programs use live online sessions — typically on Saturday and Sunday, 3–5 hours per day. Recordings are available for replay. Most programs include 1–2 campus immersion modules at the IIT campus (2–5 days each), which are scheduled with advance notice. You attend from your home or office for weekly sessions.
