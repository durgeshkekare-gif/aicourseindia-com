About IIT Bombay

IIT Bombay is India's #3 ranked institution overall and among the top 5 globally ranked Indian institutions. The Desai Sethi School of Entrepreneurship (DSSE) runs executive and entrepreneurship programs distinct from the main engineering departments. The AI for Business Managers program from DSSE is specifically designed for non-technical professionals — making IIT Bombay one of the few IITs to offer an AI program that requires no coding background.
